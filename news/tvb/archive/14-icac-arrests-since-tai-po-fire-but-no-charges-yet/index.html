14 ICAC arrests since Tai Po fire but no charges yet

14 ICAC arrests since Tai Po fire but no charges yet

發佈日期: 2025-12-17 22:54
TVB News
粵
已複製連結
Three weeks after the deadly fire at Wang Fuk Court in Tai Po, the Independent Commission Against Corruption has reportedly arrested 14 people, including the former and current chairmen of the owners' corporation, on suspicion of corruption related to the renovation work.

But none of them, along with those arrested by the police, have been charged yet. 

Tsui Moon-kum, the current chairman of the owners' corporation of Wang Fuk Court left the ICAC headquarters in North Point this afternoon, saying it isn't convenient for him to speak.

The former chairman of the Owners' Corporation, Tang Kwok-kuen also appeared at the ICAC headquarters.

They are believed to have been temporarily released on bail.
