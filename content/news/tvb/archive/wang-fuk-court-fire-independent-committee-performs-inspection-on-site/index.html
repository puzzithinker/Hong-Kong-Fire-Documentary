Wang Fuk Court fire independent committee performs inspection on site

Wang Fuk Court fire independent committee performs inspection on site

發佈日期: 2025-12-23 22:43
TVB News
粵
已複製連結
Members of the Independent Committee in relation to the Wang Fuk Court fire performed an inspection at the site of the blaze. 

Committee Chairman David Lok and fellow committee members including Executive Council member Chan Kin-por and MTR Non-executive Chairman Rex Auyeung, were seen as part of the inspection team. 

Clad in white protective clothing and helmets, they were escorted by firefighters and police officers as they approached the scene.

Sources said they entered the relatively unaffected Wang Chi House as well as one of the charred buildings and departed from the site at around noon.
